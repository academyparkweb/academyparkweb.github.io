Dr. Trista Bowyer

Trista was born in Muncie, Indiana and now lives in Highlands Ranch with her husband Todd and her 3 children, Aiden, Hailey, and Gavin. She received her medical degree from the University of Indiana School of Medicine in 2003 and completed her pediatric residency at The Children's Hospital of Los Angeles in 2006. Trista joined Academy Park Pediatrics in January of 2013. When asked about her practice philosophy she says "I strive to provide comprehensive and compassionate care to all my patients."

Trista is a board certified pediatrician and active fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics and a member of the Arapahoe County and Colorado Medical Societies.
