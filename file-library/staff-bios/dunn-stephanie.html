Dr. Stephanie Dunn

I believe that a pediatrician should help parents make informed decisions in regards to their child's health and wellness.

Stephanie is a Boston area native and attended college in Washington, DC. She graduated from New York Medical College with honors and completed her training at Columbia University's Children's Hospital. In 2010, after 10 years in NYC, she and her husband moved to Denver where Stephanie worked in the Children's Hospital of Colorado's Emergency Department. She is an active fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics. As the mother of Emmett (born May 2012) and Serena (born June 2014), Stephanie's free time is not very "free", however she enjoys all of Colorado's beautiful outdoor activities when she has a chance.
