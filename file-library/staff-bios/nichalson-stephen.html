Dr. Stephen Nichalson

Steve was born in Newport, Rhode Island and now resides in Littleton. He received his medical degree from the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center. Steve has been a board certified pediatrician since 1980, the same year he finished his residency training at the University of California, San Diego. When asked about his practice philosophy he states "To care for our patients and their families with the utmost compassion, understanding and professionalism".

Steve is an active fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics and an active member of the Arapahoe County and Colorado Medical Societies. He also enjoys educating future pediatricians as an Assistant Clinical Professor at the Children's Hospital of Colorado. As a matter of fact, Dr. Goldberg was one of Dr. Nichalson's former students.
