About Us

Currently accepting new patients at the Lakewood/Littleton and Highlands Ranch Locations!

Choosing a pediatrician to care for the medical needs of your child is a critical decision for a parent. It is important to choose someone who is qualified, caring, attentive, and approachable. You also want someone who is up-to-date on the latest developments in pediatric and adolescent medical care. At Academy Park Pediatrics these tenets hold true for each and every provider and staff person...for the last 75 years.

Our website has been designed to summarize the services we provide, keep you up to date on evolving medical issues relevant to you and your child, and even tell you a little bit about our personal selves. In addition you will find multiple links to reliable child medical care resources on the internet as well as useful routine handouts that you can print.

Patient Information

When you arrive at our office, please have your insurance card ready. Please call if you are going to be late or need to reschedule. If you have insurance or demographic changes, please arrive 5 to 10 minutes prior to your appointment.

We take all major health insurance providers and many others:

  • Aetna - Accept most products. No health exchange plans.
  • Anthem - Accept most plans. No pathways plans. Limited Blue Priority Exchange plans.
  • Cigna/Great West - Accept most products no connect plans.
  • Cofinity - Call business office to verify which coverages we accept.
  • Colorado Access - Established PTS only on a limited basis/restricted status.
  • Coventry - Call business office to verify which plans.
  • Humana - PPO only. No Exchange plans.
  • Medicaid - (Health First) not accepting additional patients/restricted status.
  • PHCS - Call business office. We accept some but not all of these plans no discount card program plans.
  • Rocky Mountain Health - We accept most products. No exchange plans.
  • Tricare - West Area only. If prime, our physician must be listed as PCM.
  • United Healthcare - Most products. No Charter, no Compass, and no Core plans.

* If your insurance carrier is not specifically listed above, you may call our insurance department at 303-988-5252.
Ask for the insurance/bookkeping department and have the information from your card ready.
We will be happy to help determine our participation.

* It is always a good idea to phone your carrier to verify our current participation status.

* We are NOT currently accepting Medicaid/Closed Practice.

Dr. Greg Arfsten

My goal is to treat others as he would want to be treated. Everybody deserves special care.

Greg is a native Coloradan, originally from Longmont, now residing in Littleton with his wife Laurie, daughter Jennifer, and sons Sean and Samuel. He received his medical degree from the University of Colorado School of Medicine where he graduated with honors. Greg then completed his pediatric residency at Denver Children's Hospital in 1993 and joined Academy Park Pediatrics as a board certified pediatrician.

Greg is an active fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics and an active member of the Arapahoe County and Colorado Medical Societies. He also gets pleasure from educating future pediatricians as an Associate Clinical Professor at the Children's Hospital of Colorado.

Greg donates his precious time at the Denver Inner City Health Center, Warren Village, and performing physical exams for participants of local Special Olympics. He is pictured here doing the things he loves... volunteering on international medical missions, fly fishing, and spending time with his family.

Dr. Michelle Rhodes

I want incorporate both parents and their children in the decision making process and encourage a team approach to care.

Michelle spent her early years in Pittsburgh and subsequently moved to Denver for the remainder of her childhood. She received her undergraduate degree from Bucknell University where she graduated Summa Cum Laude. She later attended medical school at the University of Colorado where she graduated with honors. Michelle completed her pediatric residency at The Children's Hospital of Colorado in 2006.

Michelle is a board certified pediatrician. She is an active fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics and lifetime member of Alpha Omega Alpha Medical Society. She enjoys teaching future pediatricians as an Assistant Clinical Professor at The Children's Hospital of Colorado.

Michelle has been married to her husband, Bob, since 2000 and they have two children, Nora and James. She enjoys spending time with her family, yoga, running, and travel.
